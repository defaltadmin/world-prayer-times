How to Use

  • 1. Your local time is the ruler at the top (red NOW line).
  • 2. Each row shows prayer times as colored blocks.
  • 3. Drag the cyan bar to select a meeting window. Resize by dragging edges.
  • 4. The status shows if the window conflicts with any prayer.
  • 5. Click the book icon to view Sannatayn course info and enroll.
